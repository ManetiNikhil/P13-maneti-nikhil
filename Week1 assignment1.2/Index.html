Kamareddy, INDIA

Location

Kamareddy is located 100 miles from Hyderabad

About

Kamareddy is a town and headquarters of the Kamareddy district in Telangana state in India.

It is located about 117 kilometres north of the state capital Hyderabad, 56 kilometres from Nizamabad and 96 kilometres from Karimnagar.

Weather

It's warm all year

Population

Around 80,000 people call Kamareddy home

Sports

Kabaddi and Cricket are popular sports in India
